Chicken Kebab

Delicious grilled chicken kebab served with soft pita bread and creamy garlic sauce. The chicken is marinated in aromatic spices, grilled to perfection, and paired with fresh vegetables for a satisfying meal. Perfect for lunch or dinner, this dish offers a delightful blend of flavors and textures.

Falafel Tortilla

Crispy falafel wrapped in a warm tortilla with fresh veggies and rich tahini sauce. Each bite features golden chickpea patties, crunchy lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and a drizzle of tangy sauce. This vegetarian option is both healthy and filling, making it a popular choice for all ages.
